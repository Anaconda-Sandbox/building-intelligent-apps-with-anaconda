# Who Owns Conda?

**NOT ANACONDA!** 

We gave it away and its fiscal sponsorship is maintained through NumFOCUS, and it's governance structure through `conda-forge` was specifically designed so Anaconda could not have outsized weight on the future of the tool. 

When Anaconda applies new terms of service for its users, it does not apply to `conda` the package manager, `conda` the package specification or `conda-forge` the community-led infrastructure project hosted on Anaconda.org.
