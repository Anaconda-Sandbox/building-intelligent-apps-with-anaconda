# Explain Anaconda's Terms of Service to Me Like I'm Five

## What is ELI5

ELI5 is subject matter expert terminology that encourages accessible, approachable language -- "Explain Like I'm 5". Terms of Service is a tool to communicate with users of a proprietary tool, the way one can access, use and redistribute. A Terms of Service isn't inherently a punitive measure. Having clarity on how a tool can be used protects both the user and the distributor and is best practice in the age of the [Cyber Resilliancy Act]().

Anaconda introduced a new Terms of Service in 2024, which sent waves of consideration across our +50 million users. For [Frequently asked questions visit our FAQ page]().

This readme, will explain the broad strokes of Anaconda's Terms of Service as if the reader is first being introduced to the concepts. This explanation is not legally binding document.
